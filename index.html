How It Works?

Create Account

Download the app and enter your basic information to set up your secure account. Complete your profile to access the personal dashboard where you will manage all your household expenses.

Link Residences

Enter the address for your primary residence or multiple rental units. Link your specific utility accounts to this address to centralise your billing data in one organised location.

Manage Bills

View a clear list of all your current and upcoming charges. Use the dashboard to check balances and settle your bills directly through the app to keep your accounts up to date.
