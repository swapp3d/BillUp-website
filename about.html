About Project

Managing bills across multiple platforms is often chaotic, leading to missed deadlines and manual errors. BilliO was created to bridge this gap, unifying the entire household billing experience into one seamless, transparent digital ecosystem for everyone.

Residents gain total financial clarity with one-click payments and smart alerts, while companies reduce overhead through automated billing and streamlined collections. It is efficiency for providers and peace of mind for residents in one app.
